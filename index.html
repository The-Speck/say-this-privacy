Privacy Policy

Say This - Text to Speech App

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Say This ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: Say This is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All your data stays on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Say This operates entirely on your device. The text you enter for speech synthesis is processed locally and is never sent to our servers or any third party (except as noted in the Third-Party Services section below).

Local Storage: The app stores the following preferences locally on your device:

  • Language preference
  • Voice selection
  • Autocorrect setting

This data is stored using your device's local storage and is never transmitted off your device.

Text-to-Speech Processing

Say This uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine to convert text to speech. The text you enter:

  • Is processed locally on your device
  • Is not stored permanently by the app
  • Is not transmitted to our servers

Note: Your device's text-to-speech engine may process text according to its own privacy policy (e.g., Google Text-to-Speech on Android or Apple's speech synthesis on iOS).

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We may display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data for advertising purposes according to their privacy policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location data
  • App usage data

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

iOS App Tracking Transparency

On iOS devices, we request your permission before allowing advertisers to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can change this preference at any time in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Say This is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, there is no risk of your data being compromised through our systems. Your preferences are stored locally on your device and are protected by your device's security measures.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it:

  • Access: Your settings are visible within the app
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • Portability: Settings are stored on your device and can be backed up through your device's backup system

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
